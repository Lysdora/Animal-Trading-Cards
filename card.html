Murloc

Though murlocs are relatively new to the Eastern Kingdoms, it is increasingly believed that they are actually a very ancient race of Azeroth.

  • Scientific Name: Murloc
  • Average Length: 90 centimetres
  • Average Lifespan: 12 years
  • Habitat: Forest, Mountain, Beach

The murloc is a bipedal, amphibious, semi-intelligent, aggressive race residing along coastlines, lakeshores, and riverbeds. Murlocs possess bulbous bodies, large mouths lined with rows of sharp fangs, and slime-coated skin
